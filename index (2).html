Who We Are

About Our Assembly

Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries (MFM) is a Holy Ghost–filled, prayer-focused church committed to the total liberation of men and women from every form of bondage, sickness, and oppression of the enemy.

Our assembly at SW 58 Araromi, Akure is part of the global MFM family founded by Dr. D.K. Olukoya. We believe in the power of fervent prayer, the authority of God's Word, and the reality of miracles in today's world.

We welcome everyone — seekers, believers, and families — to come and experience the fire of God. No matter where you are in your spiritual journey, there is a place for you at MFM Araromi.
